Possit nostro aeterno eu vis, ut cum quem reque

Flash Mob is a type of cultural event wherein the various societies of our college showcase their talents to support a cause. On Saturday, 11th August 2018, a Health Camp and a Flash Mob were held to spread awareness about women health issues and the benefits of regular checkups. The Flash mob took place in the G.I.P. Mall where the students and volunteers participated in spreading the awareness about women health and their sanitation. Mridul and Ayush of Fortissmo-the Music society of JIIT, sector 128 gave a spectacular performance about the importance of women's health and hygiene. There was also a power packed performance by Bhangra De Sartaj- the Indian dance society of our college. A wonderful play was acted the the talented actors of Abhivyakti-the Dramatics society. Last but not the least the students of government schools who also attended this Flash Mob were given a treat in McDonalds. Overall this was a very successful event which not only helped us in spreading awareness but also gave the students of various societies, a chance to perform for such a wonderful cause.
