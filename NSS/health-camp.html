It is health which is real wealth, not pieces of gold and silver! Good health and good sense are two of lifeâ€™s greatest blessings, they are not only responsible for calm mind but also bring inner strength and self confidence. Understanding the importance of having good health, respected professors and the volunteers of JIIT sector-62 and sector-128 joined hands with Robinhood army so as to detect health issues and intervene in time. On our 72 nd year of Independence i.e. 15 August, 2018 a free medical Health check-up camp was organized in sector- 77 ,Noida for women. The volunteers of NSS and Robinhood army started their campaign by contacting people of that area and then delivered health talks to educate them and provided information regarding proper health diet, hygiene, menstural cycle , daily eating schedule and prevention of disease, avoiding harmful substances like tobacco, alcohol etc.. Healthy habits act as effective mechanism against most illnesses and make us live a long, healthy and happy life. A regular health check-up was done which included general physical examination of women such as body weight, body mass index, blood pressure, menstural cycle and pulse rate. This entire test performed by the volunteers was interpreted and examined by the doctor. The camp got good response from people, about 33 women participated. At last the volunteers distributed biscuits and fruits to the children and had fun with them. This camp check-up was proved to be very successful under RHA guidance.
