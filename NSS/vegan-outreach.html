Possit nostro aeterno eu vis, ut cum quem reque

It takes nothing away from a human to be kind to animals To promote Veganism, a practice of abstaining from the use of animal product particularly in diet and an associated philosophy that rejects the commodity status of an animal, the NGO “Vegan Outreach” in association with Jaypee Institute of Information and Technology, Sector 128, Noida (UP) organized a publicizing campaign on 19 February 2019, from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. at the college campus itself. The volunteers Mr. Saurabh Sonkar (Coordinator at Vegan Outreach), Mr. Rahul Verma (Volunteer, Vegan Outreach) and Ms. Kuhu Goel (Student Volunteer, from Miranda House College, New Delhi) made the students watch via the Virtual Reality headsets, the autocracies which humans inflict on the livestock, and the poor state in which livestock is kept for the mere purpose of satisfying human wants for dairy products, thereby drastically reducing their life expectancy. The NSS volunteers were surprised and at the same time impressed with the determined approach of Vegan Outreach NGO volunteers, who refused to eat Rotis and Gulab Jamun served to them, at lunch, as Ghee on it was a dairy product. The volunteers also explained a sustainable method, describing how one can transform from a non-vegetarian to a vegetarian and from a vegetarian to a vegan.
